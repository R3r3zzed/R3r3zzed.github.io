Boggle Game Project

Initiating a challenging yet enriching journey, I developed a Boggle Board Solver as part of a school project. This endeavor leveraged C++ to create a solver for the classic Boggle game, where the objective is to discover words within a grid of letters. Employing advanced programming concepts such as recursion and a prefix tree (Trie), the solver excels at identifying valid words through an optimized path-finding approach. A notable achievement of this project was the enhancement of the parsing speed, enabling the processing of over 100,000 words in just 23 milliseconds. This accomplishment was achieved by refining data processing techniques, showcasing the solver's efficiency and my adeptness in algorithm optimization. Although initiated as an academic requirement, this project profoundly deepened my understanding of advanced data structures and algorithms. It stands as a testament to my dedication to solving complex problems and crafting innovative solutions.

Tron

Launching into an innovative journey, I'm developing Tron, an AI-driven personal assistant inspired by the iconic JARVIS. This endeavor, currently in its initial planning phase, seeks to integrate daily news, weather forecasts, traffic updates, and personal schedule management into a unified conversational interface. At its core, Tron will be powered by Rasa, chosen for its advanced capabilities in creating sophisticated AI interactions. While the full development cycle has yet to begin, the roadmap includes leveraging various APIs for real-time information and enhancing the assistant through comprehensive testing.
